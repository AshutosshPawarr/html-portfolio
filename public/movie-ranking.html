The Best Movies According to Ashutossh Pawar

Top 2 Movies of all time.

12th Fail

Movie is about sheer dedication by a local boy from chambhal, who fails in this 12th class examination but with his determination clears the UPSC and becomes an IPS. The characterisation and direction has been done so beautifully that one can connect with the situation. Movie gives lot of inpiration to its audience.

Super 30

Struggle of son of postman who receives scholarship from a university of USA, however, couldn't join as his family couldn't afford to buy the tickets. Starts his own coaching center allowing only 30 students in a batch from poor background and trains them to get to clear the world's most difficult entrance examination IIT Entrace. Story is about struggle and competition with the educational mafias.
